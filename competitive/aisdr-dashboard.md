## 📝 Summary

**AiSDR is a well-positioned Y Combinator-backed AI SDR platform** that has carved out a niche with:
- Deep HubSpot integration
- LinkedIn-first multichannel approach
- Unique "AI Strategist" GTM play generator
- Strong social proof (250+ clients, Fortune 500 meetings)
- Competitive pricing ($900/mo entry)

**Key gaps for Agency OS to exploit:**
- No multi-tenant/agency dashboard
- No white-label option
- Limited CRM support
- Volume caps on messaging
- No WhatsApp or true voice channels

**Visual/UX takeaway:** They hide actual product UI heavily, relying on marketing pages and testimonials. This suggests either the UI is less polished than competitors, or they're protecting IP. A cleaner, more transparent UI demo could be a differentiator.
