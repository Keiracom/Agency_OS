# Competitive Intelligence: Instantly.ai vs Smartlead.ai Dashboards

**Research Date:** 2026-01-28  
**Focus:** High-volume email platform dashboard UX

---

## Executive Summary

Both Instantly and Smartlead target agencies and sales teams needing to send email at scale. They've converged on similar feature sets but with different UX philosophies:

- **Instantly**: Clean, minimalist, "Apple-like" approach. Emphasizes simplicity and speed-to-value.
- **Smartlead**: Feature-dense, enterprise-oriented. Emphasizes power and customization.

## 7. Learnings for Agency OS

### Must-Have Features
1. **Unified Inbox (Unibox)** - Both have it; it's table stakes
2. **Unlimited accounts + warmup** - Scale without per-account fees
3. **Auto-rotation** - Across mailboxes to protect sender reputation
4. **AI categorization** - Auto-label replies by intent
5. **Pipeline/Revenue tracking** - Beyond vanity metrics

### Differentiators to Consider
1. **True Revenue Attribution** - Like Instantly, track through to closed-won
2. **White-label from Day 1** - Like Smartlead, agencies need this
3. **Built-in Lead Sourcing** - Like Instantly's Lead Finder
4. **AI Agents for GTM** - Like Smartlead's SmartAgent
5. **Subsequences by Intent** - Branching based on reply sentiment

### UX Recommendations
1. **Start minimal, reveal complexity** - Instantly's approach is more accessible
2. **Show scale visually** - Both use big numbers (accounts, emails, revenue)
3. **Campaign as primary object** - Everything flows from campaigns
4. **Unibox as command center** - Where users spend most time
5. **Progress indicators for warmup** - Visual feedback on deliverability health

### Pricing Model Insights
1. **Email volume tiers** - Both use sends/month as primary lever
2. **Add-on products** - Deliverability testing, dedicated IPs as upsells
3. **Agency/white-label tier** - Dedicated pricing for agencies
4. **No per-seat pricing** - Invite team members at no extra cost
