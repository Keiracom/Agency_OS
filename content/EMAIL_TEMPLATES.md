## ✅ Best Practices

**Keep it short**
- Initial emails: Under 100 words
- Follow-ups: Under 75 words
- LinkedIn: Under 50 words (connection), 100 words (messages)

**Subject lines**
- Lowercase preferred (feels personal)
- Use their name or company
- Curiosity > clickbait

**Tone**
- Casual but professional
- No "I hope this email finds you well"
- No "just circling back" (use "checking in" sparingly)
- "Cheers" > "Best regards"

**Timing**
- Tue-Thu mornings (9-11am local time)
- Avoid Mondays and Fridays
- Test weekend sends (sometimes work better)

**Metrics to track**
- Open rate: Target 40%+
- Reply rate: Target 5-10%
- Positive reply rate: Target 3-5%
- Meeting booked rate: Target 1-2%
