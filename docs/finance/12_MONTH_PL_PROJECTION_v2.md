### 7.3 Ad Spend Strategy by Channel

#### LinkedIn Ads (35% — $275,785)
- **Target:** Marketing agency owners/directors in AU
- **Ad Types:** Sponsored content, Lead Gen Forms, InMail
- **CPL Target:** $50-80
- **Expected Leads:** 3,400-5,500
- **Use Case:** Primary B2B lead gen

#### Google Ads (25% — $196,989)
- **Target:** High-intent keywords
- **Keywords:** "marketing agency software", "client acquisition automation", "agency CRM"
- **CPC Target:** $15-25
- **Expected Clicks:** 7,800-13,000
- **Use Case:** Capture demand

#### Meta Ads (15% — $118,194)
- **Target:** Lookalike audiences from customer list
- **Ad Types:** Video ads, carousel, lead forms
- **CPM Target:** $15-25
- **Use Case:** Awareness + retargeting pool

#### YouTube Ads (10% — $78,796)
- **Target:** Business/marketing channels
- **Ad Types:** Skippable in-stream, discovery
- **CPV Target:** $0.05-0.10
- **Expected Views:** 780,000-1,500,000
- **Use Case:** Brand building + education

#### Retargeting (10% — $78,796)
- **Platforms:** All of above + display network
- **Audiences:** Website visitors, video viewers, email openers
- **Frequency Cap:** 3-5/day
- **Use Case:** Conversion optimization

#### Sponsorships/Influencers (5% — $39,398)
- **Target:** Marketing podcasts, newsletters, YouTube channels
- **Partners:** Agency-focused content creators
- **Use Case:** Credibility + reach
