## APP 1 — Open and Transparent Management of Personal Information

### What This Policy Covers

This policy covers:
1. **Agency personal information** — information about the individuals who operate and manage Agencies using our platform
2. **Prospect personal information** — information about individual decision-makers at Australian businesses targeted by campaigns

### Kinds of Personal Information We Hold

**Agency individuals:** Name, email address, business name, ABN, billing details, LinkedIn account identifiers (via Unipile), CRM connection tokens, IP address, login activity.

**Prospect individuals (decision-makers):**
- Full name
- Job title
- LinkedIn profile URL
- Business email address (confidence-scored, sourced via Leadmagic)
- Work phone number (from GMB public listings)
- LinkedIn post content (last 90 days, public posts only)
- X/Twitter post content (last 90 days, public posts only)
- Voice call transcript and recording (if contacted by voice AI)
- Outreach interaction data (email opens, clicks, replies, LinkedIn response)

**Business-level data (not personal information for most entities, but may be for sole traders):**
- ABN, legal entity name, trading names
- Business address, phone, website
- Google My Business category, rating, review count
- LinkedIn company data (size, industry, description)
- Ad spend signals (DataForSEO)

## APP 10 — Quality of Personal Information

We take reasonable steps to ensure the personal information we hold is accurate, up-to-date, and complete:
- Prospect contact data has a 90-day cache window; stale data is re-verified at campaign time
- ABN data is refreshed monthly from the ABN bulk extract
- GMB data is refreshed quarterly on a rolling basis
- Mobile numbers are never cached — live lookup at campaign time

Despite these measures, enrichment data from third parties may be inaccurate. Our Data Usage Agreement and Terms of Service include appropriate accuracy disclaimers.

---

## APP 11 — Security of Personal Information

We take reasonable steps to protect personal information from misuse, interference, loss, and unauthorised access:

- **Database:** Supabase (PostgreSQL) on AWS Sydney (ap-southeast-2)
- **Encryption at rest:** AES-256 (Supabase default)
- **Encryption in transit:** TLS 1.2+
- **Access controls:** Row-level security enforced per Agency tenant
- **Multi-tenancy:** Cross-tenant data access is prevented at the database layer
- **Authentication:** JWT-based API authentication; OAuth for integration connections
- **Subcontractor security:** We require sub-processors to maintain appropriate security standards

When personal information is no longer needed, we destroy or de-identify it in accordance with our retention schedule.

## Do Not Call Register Act 2006 (Cth)

All telephone numbers (mobile and landline) used for outbound calling or SMS are checked against the Australian Do Not Call Register (DNCR) via ACMA API before any contact attempt:
- DNCR results are cached in Redis for a rolling period
- Numbers registered on the DNCR are permanently suppressed
- Voice AI calls are subject to the same DNCR obligations as human-initiated calls
