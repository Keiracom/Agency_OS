## 2. Service Description

Agency OS provides automated B2B client acquisition services including:

- Discovery of Australian business prospects via Google My Business, ABN data, and LinkedIn
- Prospect enrichment (contact details, company data, decision-maker identification)
- AI-powered lead scoring and prioritisation (ALS)
- Automated outreach via email, LinkedIn, SMS, and voice AI
- Campaign analytics and conversion intelligence
- CRM integration (HubSpot; additional CRMs may be added)
