# Landing Page Competitive Analysis: Agency OS

**Purpose:** Identify best-in-class landing page features from top 10 AI SDR/outbound competitors  
**Goal:** Multi-page marketing site that converts Australian marketing agencies  
**Date:** December 30, 2025

---

## Executive Summary

After analyzing 10 competitor landing pages, the winning patterns are clear:

1. **Hero messaging** focuses on outcomes (meetings, pipeline, revenue) not features
2. **Social proof** appears within first scroll (logos, metrics, testimonials)
3. **Before/After visualization** shows consolidation of tools
4. **Interactive demos** let prospects experience the product (email preview, AI chat)
5. **Pricing transparency** builds trust (even if "contact sales" for enterprise)
6. **Multi-page architecture** with dedicated pages for features, use cases, pricing

## Recommended Multi-Page Structure

### Page 1: Homepage (/)
**Purpose:** Hook + Convert
- Hero with headline + video
- Logo bar (social proof)
- Before/After visual
- 3-4 key features (visual)
- Testimonials slider
- CTA: Start Free Trial

### Page 2: How It Works (/how-it-works)
**Purpose:** Educate
- Step-by-step flow
- ICP Discovery explanation
- Campaign generation
- Multi-channel outreach
- Conversion intelligence
- Demo video (2-3 min)

### Page 3: Features (/features)
**Purpose:** Detail
- Full feature breakdown
- Organized by category:
  - Lead Discovery
  - Content Generation
  - Multi-Channel Outreach
  - Analytics & Reporting
  - Conversion Intelligence

### Page 4: Pricing (/pricing)
**Purpose:** Convert
- Clear tier comparison
- Founding tier urgency (X spots left)
- ROI calculator
- FAQ section
- CTA: Start Trial

### Page 5: For Agencies (/for-agencies)
**Purpose:** ICP-specific landing
- Australian marketing agency focus
- Pain points specific to agencies
- Case studies (when available)
- "Built for agencies like yours"

### Page 6: Waitlist/Early Access (/waitlist)
**Purpose:** Capture leads before launch
- Exclusive access messaging
- Founding tier benefits
- Progress counter (X/20 spots claimed)
- Email capture form

### Page 7: About (/about)
**Purpose:** Build trust
- Founder story (optional)
- Mission statement
- "Build in Public" philosophy
- Team (even if just you)
- Press/media (future)

### Page 8: Blog (/blog)
**Purpose:** SEO + Thought leadership
- Build in public updates
- Industry insights
- How-to guides
- Integration announcements

## Copy Framework

**Outcome-focused:**
- "Book more meetings, stress less" (AiSDR)

- "Automate your outbound" (Artisan)

**Pain-focused:**
- "Stop prospecting. Start closing." (Apollo)

**Agency OS Options:**
- "Your agency's growth on autopilot"

### CTAs That Convert

**Primary:**
- "Start Free Trial"
- "See It In Action"
- "Book a Demo"
- "Get Started"

**Secondary:**
- "Watch Demo" (video)
- "See Pricing"
- "Talk to Sales"

**Urgency:**
- "Claim Your Spot" (founding tier)
- "Only X spots left"

## Design Principles (from competitors)

### Color Palettes
- **11x:** Dark mode, purple/blue accents
- **Artisan:** Light mode, coral/peach accents
- **AiSDR:** Dark mode, blue/cyan accents
- **Apollo:** Light mode, purple accents
- **Instantly:** Light mode, blue accents

### Typography
- Large, bold headlines (48-72px)
- Clean sans-serif fonts (Inter, Plus Jakarta Sans)
- High contrast for readability

### Layout
- Full-width hero sections
- Generous whitespace
- Card-based feature displays
- Sticky navigation
- Mobile-first responsive
