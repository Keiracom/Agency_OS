### Buyer Personas

#### Persona 1: The Overwhelmed Founder
- **Name:** "Busy Ben"
- **Role:** Agency Founder/Owner
- **Pain:** Spends 60% of time on business development instead of client work
- **Fear:** Feast/famine revenue cycles
- **Goal:** Predictable pipeline without hiring salespeople
- **Objection:** "I've tried automation before, it didn't work"
- **Trigger:** Lost a big pitch, slow quarter, competitor growing faster

#### Persona 2: The Growth-Hungry Director
- **Name:** "Scaling Sarah"
- **Role:** Managing Director / Growth Lead
- **Pain:** Can't scale outreach without burning out team
- **Fear:** Hiring salespeople is expensive and risky
- **Goal:** 10x outreach volume with same headcount
- **Objection:** "AI outreach is spammy and damages brand"
- **Trigger:** Board pressure, funding milestone, expansion plans

#### Persona 3: The Tech-Forward Innovator
- **Name:** "AI Andy"
- **Role:** Agency Owner (early adopter)
- **Pain:** Knows AI is the future, can't find right tool
- **Fear:** Falling behind competitors using AI
- **Goal:** Competitive advantage through automation
- **Objection:** "Is this actually different from what I've tried?"
- **Trigger:** Saw competitor using AI, read about Artisan/11x

Based on expert panel analysis (see EXPERT_PANEL_LANDING_PAGE.md):

**Section Order:**
1. Hero (headline + CTA + social proof strip)
2. Problem (the pain of manual outreach)
3. Solution (how Agency OS works)
4. Features (5-channel, ALS, Maya)
5. Proof (testimonials, case studies, metrics)
6. Pricing (founding member offer)
7. FAQ (objection handling)
8. Final CTA (urgency + scarcity)

**Key Elements:**
- Above-the-fold demo booking
- "17 of 20 founding spots remaining" (real-time)
- Video demo embed
- Trust badges (ACMA compliant, AU data, etc.)
- ROI calculator
- Money-back guarantee

### Demo Flow

**Pre-Demo:**
1. Calendar invite with agenda
2. "Meet Maya" video intro (60 seconds)
3. Pre-demo questionnaire (ICP, challenges, goals)

**Demo Script (15 min):**
1. **Intro (2 min):** "What made you book this call?"
2. **Discovery (3 min):** Current outreach process, pain points
3. **Demo (7 min):** Walk through Agency OS solving their specific pain
4. **Pricing (2 min):** Present founding offer
5. **Next Steps (1 min):** Close or schedule follow-up

**Post-Demo:**
1. Summary email within 1 hour
2. Personalized proposal if interested
3. 48-hour follow-up if no response
4. Nurture sequence if not ready
