## Collection Process

### Questions for Customer Interview

**Background:**
1. Tell me about {{company_name}} ‚Äî what do you do, who do you serve?
2. How long have you been in business?
3. What's your team size and structure?

**The Problem:**
4. What was business development like before Agency OS?
5. How many hours/week did you spend on sales activities?
6. What was your typical lead flow ‚Äî meetings per month?
7. What tools or approaches had you tried before?
8. What specifically wasn't working?
9. Was there a breaking point or trigger that made you look for a solution?

**The Solution:**
10. How did you discover Agency OS?
11. What made you decide to try it vs. alternatives?
12. What was the implementation/onboarding experience like?

**The Results:**
13. What changes did you notice first?
14. What are your current meetings/month?
15. How much time are you saving?
16. How many new clients have you signed since starting?
17. What's the revenue impact?
18. Any unexpected benefits?

**Wrap-Up:**
19. What advice would you give other agencies considering Agency OS?
20. What are your plans for the future with Agency OS?
21. Can I quote you directly? Any phrases you'd like to be quoted on?
