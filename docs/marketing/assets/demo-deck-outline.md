## Demo Best Practices

### Before the Call
- [ ] Research the company (LinkedIn, website, recent news)
- [ ] Pre-load their ICP in the demo account
- [ ] Test screen share, audio, video
- [ ] Have proposal template ready

### During the Call
- [ ] Start with discovery ‚Äî don't pitch immediately
- [ ] Use their words back to them
- [ ] Demo to their pain, not all features
- [ ] Watch for buying signals (leaning in, detailed questions)
- [ ] Handle objections with questions, not arguments
- [ ] Always ask for the next step

### After the Call
- [ ] Send summary email within 1 hour
- [ ] Send proposal if they were warm/hot
- [ ] Log notes in CRM
- [ ] Schedule follow-up if needed
