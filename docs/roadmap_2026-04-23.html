Core Design Principle

NO TEMPLATES — ALL LLM-GENERATED

Every outreach message — email, LinkedIn, voice script — is uniquely generated by LLM per prospect. The operator never writes messages. They configure an Agency Voice Profile (tone, services, differentiators) and the AI generates personalised content using prospect signals, VR grades, and enrichment data. This is Agency OS's core differentiator vs template-based competitors (Instantly, Smartlead, AiSDR). Three-way matching: prospect signals × agency capabilities × channel format.
