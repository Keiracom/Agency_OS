Your Safety Net

Every action is
governed.

These rules are active from the moment your workforce starts. They emerged from 12 months of real operational failures. You don't configure them — they just work.

Active Governance 17 laws active
🛑
Kill Switch — one click pauses every agent instantly. Always visible. Always available. No delay.
👁️
Approval Queue — every action your agents take appears in your queue first. You approve, reject, or edit before anything executes.
💰
Budget Controls — daily spending cap. If agents exceed 5× the estimate, the system kills itself automatically. No runaway costs.
🔒
Graduated Trust — every agent starts at Level 1 (read only). You promote them as trust builds. Level 4 (autonomous) is earned, not given.
👥
Peer Correction — agents check each other's work. If one agent makes a mistake, another catches it before it reaches you.
📋
Audit Trail — every action, every decision, every cost is logged. Fully traceable. Nothing is hidden.
🚫
Fabrication Protection — agents cannot claim work they haven't done or reference results that don't exist. Hard-blocked at the system level.
🔐
Tool Isolation — each agent can only access the tools assigned to its role. The bookkeeper can't touch your email. The comms agent can't touch your books.
