How it works

Not a tool you operate.
An operator that runs your agency.

The data sources are public infrastructure — ABN registry, Google Maps, LinkedIn. The intelligence is the algorithm: knowing which signals mean buying intent, for which prospect, at which moment. Our agents work from that algorithm continuously — surfacing the best-fit prospects for your agency at the exact moment they need you.

01 · FIND

Builds your prospect pool continuously.

Sweeps Google Maps, the ABN registry, and LinkedIn across your target industries and locations — 2.4 million AU businesses in the index, with every signal pulled fresh from the source at discovery. No list-building. No cached databases. No manual research.

02 · READ

Reads 15+ buying signals per prospect.

Ad spend, hiring activity, review trends, founder posts, website health, expansion markers. Every signal weighted. Every prospect scored for propensity and reachability in real time.

03 · RUN

Sequences across four channels.

Email, LinkedIn, SMS, and voice AI — timed, personalised, coordinated. Every message reviewed and approved by you before it sends. Manual control first, autopilot only when you trust it.

The hard questions

Straight answers.

"Am I buying an operator or a part-time job?"
You review your top ten messages — each one unique, hyper-personalised, and written in your voice. When you're ready, release the batch. Review more first if you want. Month two onward: one button per cycle. You're approving, not writing.
"What if this gets my primary domain blacklisted?"
It can't. Outreach never sends from your primary domain. Every customer gets dedicated burner domains with automated warm-up, rotation, and Australian sending infrastructure. Your reputation is yours. Ours is on the line.
"Is this actually live — or scraped from a stale database?"
The ABN registry is a local join for instant filtering. Every other signal — Google Maps, LinkedIn, ad activity, review trends, website health — is pulled fresh from the source the moment a prospect enters your cycle. Nothing cached. Nothing stale.
