Step 01 of 05 — Connect your CRM

Let's protect your existing clients first.

Agency OS reads your HubSpot so we know exactly who to exclude from outreach. Your clients, your active deals, your recent contacts — all protected from the moment we start. We also write booked meetings back into your pipeline so nothing lives outside your system of record.

What Agency OS does with HubSpot
  • ReadContacts, companies, deals. Used to build your exclusion list — we never cold-outreach anyone already in your pipeline.
  • ReadDeal history. Tells us which services actually make you money, not what your website says.
  • WriteNew contacts from booked meetings. When a prospect books, the contact appears in your HubSpot automatically.
  • WriteDeal + calendar event + briefing attachment. Every booked meeting lands in your HubSpot workflow, ready for your sales process.
Secure connection — we never see your HubSpot password. Revoke access any time.
Step 02 of 05 — Connect your LinkedIn

Your LinkedIn — used as yours.

Agency OS sends connection requests and messages from your real LinkedIn account. Your name, your profile, your presence. This is the honest disclosure of what we do with it — every action it takes and every limit we respect to keep your account safe.

What Agency OS does with your LinkedIn
  • ReadYour profile and recent posts — to match your agency's voice when writing outreach on your behalf.
  • ReadYour connections — as an exclusion list. We never cold-message someone you already know.
  • WriteConnection requests — from your account to prospects we identify. Personalised, timed naturally, within conservative limits below LinkedIn's own guidelines.
  • WriteFollow-up messages — after a connection is accepted, coordinated with your email and voice AI sequences.

Every action comes from you, not from Agency OS. We operate well below LinkedIn's published limits, with randomised delays and business-hours-only activity. You can pause or revoke access any time.

We'll run a quick account quality check after connecting to make sure your LinkedIn is ready for outreach.
