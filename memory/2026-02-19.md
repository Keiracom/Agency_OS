3. **Gate triggers:**

   - **Gate 2:** no email AND no phone, ALS <35 after T3, Hunter confidence <70%
   - **Gate 3:** demote tier only, never discard

4. **Batch controller flow:**
   - Monitors quota per campaign
   - Triggers replacement GMB discovery on discard
   - Loops until quota of ALS ≥35 leads met
   - Fires quota_shortfall alert after 3 failed loops
