### Part C — Batch Controller & Discard Loop
**New files:** `supabase/migrations/056_discarded_leads.sql`, `src/orchestration/flows/batch_controller_flow.py`

1. **Database tables created:**
   - `discarded_leads` - tracks lead_id, discard_gate (1/2/3), discard_reason, als_at_discard, held_until
   - `admin_notifications` - for alerts (angry_complaint, quota_shortfall, booking_confirmed)
   - `campaign_quota_status` - quota monitoring per campaign

2. **Helper functions:**
   - `soft_discard_lead()` - creates discard record, sets pool_status='discarded_pending'
   - `hard_delete_expired_discards()` - removes leads after 48hr hold period
   - `create_admin_notification()` - creates admin alerts

3. **Gate triggers:**
   - **Gate 1:** ICP fail, invalid ABN, duplicate, pre-ALS <35 (free tier only)
   - **Gate 2:** no email AND no phone, ALS <35 after T3, Hunter confidence <70%
   - **Gate 3:** demote tier only, never discard

4. **Batch controller flow:**
   - Monitors quota per campaign
   - Triggers replacement GMB discovery on discard
   - Loops until quota of ALS ≥35 leads met
   - Fires quota_shortfall alert after 3 failed loops
