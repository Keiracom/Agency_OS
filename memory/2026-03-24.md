### Key Design Decisions
- **is_advertiser signal (+25 points):** YP paid listing = business already in "I pay for customer acquisition" mode
- **Review sweet spot 5-80:** Established but not dominant, still needs marketing help
- **Years 2-8 sweet spot:** Owner still hands-on, scaling phase
- **Gap gate at stage 8 (≥60):** Prevents Leadmagic spend on unqualified leads
- **BD GMB SERP demoted to stage 7 enrichment** (not discovery) — saves ~70% of current BD spend
- **Claude Haiku for personalisation** ($0.003/lead vs $0.075 for Sonnet) — constrained task doesn't need frontier model
- **LinkedIn posts = personalisation fuel** — last 5 posts tell you what DM cares about
