## Vapi/ElevenLabs Realism Research [SELF]

Dave asked: "Will leads know they're talking to AI?"

**Findings:**
- ElevenLabs at/near "Turing test threshold" (~50% can't tell difference)
- ElevenLabs CEO announced crossing threshold late 2025/early 2026
- Vapi achieves 500-800ms response latency
- Key features: interrupt detection, backchanneling ("okay", "sure"), emotion detection

**What gives it away:** Unnatural pauses, too polished (no "ums"), can't handle chaos

**Bottom line:** Most leads won't know on a 3-minute cold call if tuned well.
