## Decision 2: Facebook Rejected as Discovery Source, Approved as Posts Layer
**Date:** 2026-03-01
**Decision:** Do not add Facebook as Discovery Source 3. Add Facebook page posts as T-DM4 personalization signal.

## Decision 3: ALS Split into Reachability + Propensity
**Date:** 2026-03-01
**Decision:** Replace single ALS score with dual-score model: Reachability (100 pts) + Propensity (100 pts).
**Rationale:** Reachability measures contact data quality (can we reach them?). Propensity measures likelihood to convert (should we reach them?). Separation allows independent optimization and clearer gating logic.

## Decision 4: Poor Digital Presence as Positive Signal
**Date:** 2026-03-01
**Decision:** Score businesses with poor digital presence higher in Propensity, not lower.
**Rationale:** Agency OS sells digital services. A business with weak online presence is MORE likely to need and buy services, not less. Inverts traditional lead scoring logic.

## Decision 5: Service-Aware Propensity Weights from Onboarding ICP
**Date:** 2026-03-01
**Decision:** Propensity scoring weights derived from client's ICP selections during onboarding.
**Rationale:** A web design agency and an SEO agency have different ideal customers. Service selection during onboarding configures which signals matter most for that client's Propensity calculation.
