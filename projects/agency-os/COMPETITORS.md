## AI SDR Market Overview

The AI SDR space is crowded with tools promising automated sales development. Most focus on:
- Email volume and sequences
- Basic personalization
- Activity metrics (sends, opens, replies)

Agency OS differentiates through:
- **Outcome focus** (show rate, deals closed)
- **Australian market specialization**
- **Agency B2B model** (not direct enterprise sales)
- **Proprietary ALS scoring** (predictive, not just intent signals)

## Competitive Dashboard Analysis

*What competitors show vs. what Agency OS should show*

### Common Patterns (Avoid)
- Activity metrics (emails sent, calls made) — vanity
- Technical health indicators — operator concerns
- Complex funnels with many stages — overwhelming

### Best Practices (Adopt)
- Hero metric front and center
- Trend indicators (↑↓)
- Actionable next steps
- "What's working" insights
- Upcoming calendar/meetings

### Agency OS Dashboard Advantage
Focus on what a $7,500/month customer actually cares about:
1. "Am I getting meetings?" (Meetings vs Goal)
2. "Is my pipeline growing?" (Pipeline value, ROI)
3. "Who should I talk to today?" (Hot prospects)
4. "What's coming up?" (Scheduled meetings)
5. "What's working?" (ONE clear insight)
