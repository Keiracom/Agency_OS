Create an Instagram-style carousel component for a "How It Works" section on a SaaS landing page.

DESIGN REQUIREMENTS:
- Dark theme with text-white on dark background
- Instagram/story-style carousel with 5 slides
- Current slide is fully visible and in focus
- Previous and next slides are partially visible on the sides, blurred/out of focus (opacity-50, blur-sm, scale-95)
- Large left/right arrow buttons for navigation
- Dot indicators at bottom showing current position
- Auto-advances every 6 seconds (pauses on hover)

HEADER:
- Small blue text: "How It Works" (uppercase, tracking-wider)
- Large headline: "From zero to booked meetings"
- Subheadline: "In days, not months." (text-white/50)

5 CAROUSEL SLIDES (each slide has two columns):

SLIDE 1 - Discover:
- Badge: "5 minutes" (blue)
- Title: "Enter your website URL"
- Description: "Our AI scans your services, case studies, and testimonials. It extracts exactly who your ideal clients are — industries, company sizes, decision-maker titles."
- Subtext: "No forms. No questionnaires. Just your URL."
- Right side: Mock UI showing URL input and extracted ICP data (industries, company size, titles)

SLIDE 2 - Scout:
- Badge: "Automated" (emerald)
- Title: "AI scouts your leads"
- Description: "Our enrichment engine finds prospects matching your ICP. Verified emails, direct dials, LinkedIn profiles."
- Subtext: "Hundreds of leads, ready to reach."
- Right side: List of 3 lead cards with avatars, names, titles, "Verified" badges

SLIDE 3 - Score:
- Badge: "Intelligent" (orange)
- Title: "Agency Lead Score (ALS)"
- Description: "Not all leads are equal. Our ALS scores every prospect across 5 dimensions."
- Subtext: "Hot leads get priority. Cold leads get nurtured."
- Right side: ALS score card showing score of 92 with 5 dimension progress bars

SLIDE 4 - Reach:
- Badge: "24/7" (purple)
- Title: "Multi-channel outreach begins"
- Description: "Email → LinkedIn → SMS → Voice AI → Direct Mail. All orchestrated automatically."
- Subtext: "Your voice, amplified across every channel."
- Right side: Multi-channel sequence visualization (email→wait→linkedin→wait→voice)

SLIDE 5 - Convert:
- Badge: "Results" (emerald)
- Title: "Meetings land in your calendar"
- Description: "When prospects reply, we capture the win, book the meeting, and update your pipeline."
- Subtext: "You focus on closing. We handle everything else."
- Right side: "Meeting Booked!" success card with conversion insights

NAVIGATION:
- Left arrow: large circular button with chevron, positioned on left edge
- Right arrow: large circular button with chevron, positioned on right edge
- Both arrows should have hover effects
- Dot indicators below carousel (5 dots, active one is wider/gradient)

STYLING:
- Cards use bg-[#12121a] with border border-white/10, rounded-2xl
- Demo panels use bg-[#0a0a0f]
- Side slides have: opacity-50, blur-sm, scale-95, pointer-events-none
- Use Tailwind CSS and lucide-react icons
- Smooth transitions (duration-500 ease-out)
